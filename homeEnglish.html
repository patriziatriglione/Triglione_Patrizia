Patrizia Triglione - Web Designer & Full Stack Developer


About me

Hi everyone! My name is Patrizia Triglione and I'm a Web Designer aiming to become a Full Stack Developer.
Since I was a child I have always been fascinated by the digital world, I remember with my first pc,
I spent the afternoons learning the various programs and writing random texts to be able to practice writing with 10 fingers and as fast as possible!

Education

During middle school, he studied various Microsoft Office programs and, during high school, I chose the Accountant-Programmer address.
During the 5 years of high school I began to approach the world of the web, studying HTML e CSS using Adobe Dreamweaver.
Also, I began to immerse myself in the world of design by learning how to useAdobe Photoshop.
In addition to the website, I undertook the Cisco Networking Academy Program for two years, a training program by Cisco Systems.
In addition to all this, I also studied the Visual Basic which, even if it is now a language that hasn't been used for years,
it allowed me to approach the world of programming.

Transfer

Not being able to continue my studies, I decided to move to Germany to be able to work a little, save money and thus be able to resume my studies.
For a while I concentrated on studying German and, after 2 years of working in a restaurant,
I was hired by DHL/Deutsche Post where I currently work, but my goal is quite another.

Web Designer

Thanks to my work I resumed my studies, especially deepening the part of the web and graphics.
I attended Web Designer e Adobe Photoshop courses, also studying some Marketing e Seo.
I recently passed the Adobe Dreamweaver exam and am now aiming for actual programming.
With the discovery of Start2Impact University, I started the path of Full Stack Developer and, thanks to the practical projects to be delivered at the end of each module,
I'm learning a lot about the world of programming.

It's still...

As you understand, I'm a determined girl who doesn't give up on her goals, even if they seem difficult to achieve.
Besides programming and graphics, I really enjoy reading and writing (which makes me quite imaginative), photography and traveling.
Ah yes, I also like video games and TV series marathons!
